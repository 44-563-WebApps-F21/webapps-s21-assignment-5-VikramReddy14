    Yak

    The domestic yak is a long-haired domesticated cattle found throughout the Himalayan region of the Indian subcontinent, the Tibetan Plateau, Northern Myanmar, Yunnan, Sichuan and as far north as Mongolia and Siberia. It is descended from the wild yak.

    Antelope

    The term antelope is used to refer to many species of even-toed ruminant that are indigenous to various regions in Africa and Eurasia. Antelope comprise a wastebasket taxon within the family Bovidae, encompassing all Old World ruminants that are not bovines, caprines, deer, or giraffes.

    Dolphin

    Dolphin is the common name of aquatic mammals within the infraorder Cetacea. The term dolphin usually refers to the extant families Delphinidae, Platanistidae, named Iniidae, and Pontoporiidae, and the extinct Lipotidae. There are 40 extant species named as dolphins.
